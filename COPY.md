## TONE OF VOICE

Warm. Direct. Professional.

NOT: Corporate, medical, casual, preachy, 
     salesy, buzzword-heavy

ALWAYS: Clear in one read, respectful of the 
        user's intelligence, specific over vague,
        action-oriented

READ EVERY LINE AS:
- A 52-year-old PSW from Brampton on her phone
- A busy agency coordinator between client visits
- A family member anxious about their parent

If any of them would be confused — rewrite it.

---

## WORDS WE NEVER USE
- Leverage / Leveraging
- Synergy
- Seamless
- Revolutionary (save for investor decks)
- Cutting-edge
- World-class
- Best-in-class
- Robust
- Empower (overused in care sector)
- Screens / Recommends (non-recommender liability)
- Best fit / Perfect match (implies guarantee)
- Delivers professionals (implies staffing agency)
- Verified by Careified (we don't verify — we present)

## WORDS WE ALWAYS USE
- Reputation (earned, visible, verifiable)
- Aligned / Alignment score (not match score in copy)
- Criteria (agencies set them, caregivers meet them)
- Portable (caregiver profile travels with them)
- Professional (caregiving is a profession)
- Transparent (families see, agencies see, caregivers see)
- Reliable signals (what agencies actually need)
