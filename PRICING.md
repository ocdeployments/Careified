## AGENCY PRICING (B2B)

### Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Starter | $99 CAD/mo | Dashboard, search, profiles, shortlist, roster (20 caregivers), client intake |
| Growth | $249 CAD/mo | Everything in Starter + AIRecruit, CSV import, family portal |
| Scale | $499 CAD/mo | Everything in Growth + unlimited caregivers, AI assistant, white label |
| Enterprise | Custom | API access, custom integrations, dedicated support SLA |

### Early Adopter Pricing
- First 2 test agencies (Ontario + Texas): **Free for 3 months** on Growth tier
- After 3 months: Convert to paid or downgrade to Starter

### Per-Use Costs
- AIRecruit calls: $0.12/min (built into Growth/Scale tiers)
- Family portal: $10 CAD/mo per client family (Growth tier includes 10)

## CAREGIVER PRICING (B2C)

### Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Free | $0 | Basic profile, visible in search |
| Professional | $9.99 CAD/mo | Profile analytics (views, shortlists), WhatsApp notifications, placement alerts, priority in search |
| Elite | $19.99 CAD/mo | Everything in Professional + AI career assistant (WhatsApp), direct agency messaging, credential expiry reminders, profile strength coaching |

### Revenue Model
- Two-sided: agencies pay for platform, caregivers pay for premium features
- Both sides must see value within first session
